Get Started with Evaria

Everything you need to launch your own powerful, offline-ready event app in minutes.

How to Get Started

Getting started with Evaria is easy. Follow these simple steps to create your custom event app without writing a single line of code.

1 Create Your Account

Sign up for free. No credit card, no commitment—just instant access to the builder.

2 Set Up Your Event

Add your event name, logo, dates, and branding. This sets the tone and structure of your app.

3 Build Your Content

Add stages, acts, vendors, or any type of directory item. Use the intuitive admin interface to organize everything clearly.

4 Map It Out

Drop pins, assign geolocations, and build your event map for easy navigation.

5 Schedule It

Assign times to your listings and create a fully functional event timetable. Attendees can star what they care about.

6 Go Live

Publish your app instantly. Share it via a link or QR code—no app store approval needed.
