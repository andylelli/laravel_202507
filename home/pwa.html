Progressive Web App

Why Evaria skips the app store—and why that's a good thing.

Evaria is a PWA

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are modern web applications that behave like native apps—but run entirely through a browser. They load fast, work offline, and can be added to your home screen with a single tap.


Unlike traditional apps, PWAs don't need to go through an app store. That means no gatekeeping, no lengthy approval process, no forced updates, and no corporate restrictions. You're in control—Evaria lets you publish instantly and share your app via a simple link or QR code.

Works Offline

Once it’s loaded, it keeps working—even in airplane mode or low signal areas.

Installable

One tap adds the app to your home screen. No installs. No updates.

Real-Time Updates

Changes from the admin app go live immediately for your audience.

Fast & Lightweight

Loads quickly and uses minimal data—ideal for festival environments.

No App Store Needed

No approvals, no rejections. You're live when you're ready.

Privacy Respecting

No data mining, no background tracking—your users stay in control.
