Tools for Organizers

Quickly build and manage your own event app—no coding, no headaches. Just powerful tools that work.

Built for Organizers Too

Evaria isn’t just built for attendees—it’s designed with organizers in mind. The admin backend makes setting up and managing your event app fast, clear, and stress-free.

Set Up in Minutes

Add your branding, event dates, and theme colors to instantly bring your event app to life.

Add Content that Matters

Use smart directories to add acts, vendors, stages, and facilities. Group them using parent/child links for intuitive navigation.

Geolocation

Place pins on your event map to show where everything is—like stages, toilets, or food trucks. This adds location info to each item so attendees can use GPS directions to find things easily.

Build the Schedule

Add times to your listings—like performances or activities—and Evaria turns it into a clear, scrollable event schedule for your audience.

Share with Ease

Distribute your app instantly with a link or QR code. No installs, no downloads, no friction.

Push Live Updates

Send real-time news, updates, or changes directly to your users as they happen.
